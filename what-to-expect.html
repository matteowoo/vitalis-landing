Your Journey

What to expect when you join Vitalis.

From your first consultation to a fully personalised 90-day protocol — here is every step of the process.

1

Book Your Initial Consultation

The process begins with a booking through the contact form or by phone. A care coordinator reaches out within 24 hours to schedule your initial consultation at the Osborne Park clinic.

Online booking 24-hour response No joining fee
2

Comprehensive Baseline Testing

On your first visit, the clinical team runs the full Vitalis baseline battery — a suite of imaging, bloodwork, and functional assessments that map your complete biological landscape.

🔬

DEXA Body Composition Scan

Gold-standard imaging for lean muscle mass, body fat percentage, bone mineral density, and visceral fat measurement. Tracks changes in body composition across each 90-day cycle.

Beyond Health
🧹

MRI Structural Imaging

Detailed imaging for joint, soft tissue, and spinal assessment. Used to identify structural issues, injury risk, and musculoskeletal conditions that inform your allied health and training plan.

Merse Wellness
🩸

Advanced Blood Panel — 60+ Biomarkers

Comprehensive bloodwork covering hormonal profiles (cortisol, testosterone, oestrogen, DHEA, thyroid), micronutrient status (iron, B12, vitamin D, zinc, magnesium), inflammatory markers (CRP, homocysteine), and metabolic health (insulin sensitivity, HbA1c, lipid fractions).

Beyond Health
❤️

HRV + Nervous System Baseline

Heart rate variability testing to assess autonomic nervous system function, stress resilience, and recovery capacity. Used to calibrate training intensity, cold/heat therapy dosing, and recovery protocols.

Beyond Health
🏃

Movement Screen + Structural Assessment

Functional assessment of gait, joint mobility, postural symmetry, and biomechanics. Determines which allied health practitioners — chiropractic, physiotherapy, EP, podiatry — are prescribed into your protocol from day one.

Beyond Health
3

Data Analysis + Blueprint Creation

Your clinical team analyses every data point — bloodwork, imaging, body composition, movement, and HRV — to build your individual blueprint. No two blueprints are the same. Every intervention is calibrated to what your data reveals, not population averages.

4

Receive Your Personalised Plans

You receive a complete suite of coordinated plans, all derived from your data and managed by your integrated care team.

Workout Plan

Periodised strength and conditioning at Fitness Cartel, built from your body composition and movement data.

Fitness Cartel

Allied Health Plan

Chiropractic, physiotherapy, exercise physiology, and podiatry sessions prescribed proactively based on your structural findings.

Beyond Health

GP Plan

Clinical oversight, specialist referrals, and prescription management.

Beyond Health

Supplement Protocol

A targeted supplement stack derived directly from your bloodwork.

Beyond Health

Nutrition Prescription

Personalised micro and macronutrient targets with a weekly meal plan, derived from your biomarkers and body composition.

Beyond Health
5

90-Day Protocol Execution

Your protocol is live. Training sessions at Fitness Cartel, allied health appointments, wellness modalities — cold, heat, infrared, hyperbaric — and nutrition are all coordinated around your data and recovery capacity. Your care team monitors your progress throughout.

6

Mid-Protocol Review + Retest (Day 45)

At the halfway mark, key metrics are rechecked. The team assesses your response to interventions and adjusts supplement stacks, training loads, modality dosing, and allied health frequency as needed. This is not a passive check-in — it is a data-driven recalibration.

7

End-of-Protocol Reassessment + Iteration (Day 90)

Full reassessment repeating the baseline battery — blood panel, DEXA, HRV, movement screen. Your results are compared against your original data. A new 90-day protocol is built from the updated picture. The cycle continues and compounds.

Repeat bloodwork DEXA rescan HRV reassessment Protocol updated
