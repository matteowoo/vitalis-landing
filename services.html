The Structural Foundation

Before anything else, the body must move and function.

Movement is the foundation. Allied health practitioners are embedded at the centre of every client protocol from day one — not a referral afterthought. Chiropractic, physiotherapy, exercise physiology, and podiatry are prescribed proactively.

Podiatry

Gait deviation · Lower limb injury · Training load increase
